Resume Builder

An online tool that helps you create a resume using pre-designed templates.

A resume builder is an online tool that helps job seekers create a professional resume without starting from a blank Word document. Good resume builders offer pre-designed templates, section-by-section guidance, real-time preview, and one-click PDF export.

Not all resume builders are genuinely free. Many use a dark pattern: you fill in all your information for free, then hit a paywall at the download step. The few that are truly free either ask for an account, add a watermark, or limit your exports. Resume88 is an exception — no signup, no credit card, no watermark, unlimited exports, and the code is open source.
