Template (Resume)

A pre-designed resume layout you can fill in with your information.

A resume template is a pre-designed layout — fonts, spacing, colors, structure — that you fill in with your own content. Templates save you from formatting from scratch and ensure your resume looks professional. They come in many styles: classic, modern, minimal, creative, corporate, and so on.

The best resume templates are ATS-friendly (single-column, standard fonts, no tables or text boxes) and subtle in design — they support your content without stealing attention from it. Avoid templates with colorful sidebars, skill bars, or graphic elements if you're applying to corporate roles; save those for creative industries.
